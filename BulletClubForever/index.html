I'm going to start trying to document my progress here in the hope that this will somehow mean that I don't abandon this after two weeks like I have all my other games I've tried to make. So far I've got a randomised level map which is infinitely less clever than Spelunky's but I guess inifinitely less clever than perfection isn't that bad. The map is created by this class which also has the capability of adding to itself as the player progresses through the level. I mean to make this a bit clevererer by having the array recycle itself instead of just getting bigger and bigger. The game does have the word forever in the title after all.

Phaser draws sprites/groups in the order that they're added to the game, so I managed to make the grass appear in front of the player, but I think I'm going to abaondon clever draw orders for now and concentrate on getting the basics in place. In case you hadn't noticed, it's not a game yet rather than a highly complex way of drawing pictures on a web page that took ages.
